Biography

I am a recent physics graduate from South Africa (now living in the UK) looking for an entry-level role as a data analyst.

During my university studies, I took subjects like probability theory, statistics, computer science, scientific computing and computational physics. In these and other subjects, I learnt to use tools like Excel and Python (mostly NumPy and matplotlib) to analyse scientific data from experiments and simulations.

I graduated in December 2024 and decided to make a career switch to data analytics since it was an area which I enjoyed greatly during my degree. Since then, I have relocated to the UK and learnt tools like MySQL and Power BI while consolidating my knowledge of Excel.
